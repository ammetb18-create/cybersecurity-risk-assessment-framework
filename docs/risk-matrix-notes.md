# Risk Matrix Notes

## Risk Estimation Model

Risk is estimated using the following formula:

**Risk = Probability × Impact**

Where:

- **Probability** represents the likelihood of a threat occurring.
- **Impact** represents the potential damage to the organization.

Both factors are evaluated on a **scale from 1 to 5**.

## Risk Levels

| Score | Risk Level |
|------|-------------|
| 1–4 | Low |
| 5–9 | Medium |
| 10–15 | High |
| 16–25 | Critical |

## Example

| Asset | Threat | Probability | Impact | Risk |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|------|
| Financial Database | Ransomware | 4 | 5 | 20 |
| Employee Accounts | Phishing | 4 | 4 | 16 |
| Cloud Infrastructure | Service Failure | 3 | 4 | 12 |

## Purpose

This model helps organizations visualize cybersecurity risks and prioritize mitigation strategies.
